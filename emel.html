Frequently Asked Questions

Questions about orders, delivery, custom cakes, payments and allergens. Click a question to reveal the answer.

Steps: Choose items on the Menu page → Click ORDER → Adjust quantity in the modal → Add to cart → Checkout. For custom cakes, contact us with details and we’ll quote lead time and price.

We offer pickup and delivery via partners in nearby areas. Delivery cost depends on distance — check at checkout or contact us for a quote. Orders placed close to opening hours may have limited delivery slots.

We accept custom cake orders for events. Typical lead time is 3–7 days depending on complexity. Provide size, flavors and a reference image; we'll reply with a quote and confirmation. A deposit may be required.

We accept cash at pickup, popular e-wallets, and bank transfers for large orders. For custom orders we may request a deposit. Payment instructions are provided at checkout or in confirmation.

We use ingredients such as wheat, dairy and eggs. For allergy-sensitive customers, tell us before ordering and we’ll advise or suggest alternatives where possible. Note: cross-contact may still happen in our kitchen.
